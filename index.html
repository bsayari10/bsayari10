About Me

Hey, This is Sayari, I belong to a small town Chinsurah on the bank of Hooghly River. Since I was little, I have enjoyed playing sports (like football and cricket), listening to music, reading, and creating artwork. My tenth and twelfth grades were completed at Chinsurah Balika Bani Mandir. I received a 97% in the Madhyamik 2016 exam, placing me fifth overall in West Bengal. In 2018, I received a higher secondary grade point average of 95.8%. I then enrolled in the Physics Department at Jadavpur University. I earned a Bachelor's degree in 2021 including an 8.77 cgpa. I have earned my master's degree at SNBNCBS with 86% marks and doing a project in computational Biophysics. The three years I spent in Jadavpur (2018-2021) were some of the happiest in my life. Lionel Messi has a fanatic fan in me. Since I was 14 years old, I have regarded him as my hero. December 18, 2022, was undoubtedly the happiest day of my life.

I have already utilized Molecular Dynamics simulations to investigate stability of Amyloid fibril systems, focusing on rare event sampling.Employed Metadynamics to explore and sample freeenergy landscapes,providing deeper insights into the stability and aggregation mechanisms of Amyloid oligomers.
